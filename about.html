Intro to the Game

In ULTARKILL you play as a robot named V1. Descend through the levels of hell and you take out every husk, demon, machine, and even angel in your way. Fight with insteresting mechanics and move like your speeding.

Mechanics

Movement

While this game has an arsenal of weapons, it also has an arsenal of movement options, such as:

  • Jumping
  • Wall Jumping
  • Wall Sliding
  • Dashing
  • Dash Jumping
  • Sliding
  • Slide Jumping
  • Ground Slam
  • Skimming (Sliding on water)
  • Rocket Surfing
  • Rocket Jumping
  • And using any kind of explosive weapon to get varying speed boosts

    • Weapons

    As of the current version () there are 5 weapons with an average of 2 variations per weapon.

  • Revolver
  • Shotgun
  • Nailgun
  • Railgun
  • Rocket Launcher

    • Combine weapon alternate fires for deadly and stylish combos.

    Style Meter

    The Style Meter is a mechanic that that shows a letter grade depending on your style. Progress through letters D,C,B,A,S,SS,SSS, and ULTRAKILL to achieve style points to reach Perfect rank on that level. Finishing off enemies with different weapons, ricochets, punches, and more to rank up style.

    The soundtrack

    "A Shattered Illusion" conveys exactly what its name entails.

    "A Complete and Utter Destruction of the Senses" perfectly portrays the level it takes place in, "A Burning World".

    "Le Castlevein" starts off instantly, no filler or calming music. The layer of Hell known as Limbo is trying as hard as it can to stop you throwing more enemies than ever at you.

    "Claude Debussy: Claire De Lune" plays right before the final fight of Limbo. It encapsules the feeling of "calm before the storm".

    Finally, "Versus" starts right as a red robot known as V2 enters a church. This iconic song plays for the fisrt and last time you fight V2.
